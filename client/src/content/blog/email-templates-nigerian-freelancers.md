Freelancing in Nigeria means managing client communication almost entirely by yourself. These 10 templates cover the most common scenarios — adapt them for your tone and situation.

## 1. Initial project inquiry response

When a potential client reaches out:

---
Subject: Re: [Their subject / project inquiry]

Dear [Name],

Thank you for reaching out. I've reviewed your project details and I believe I can help.

Quick questions to ensure I scope this accurately:
- What's your target launch date?
- Do you have existing brand materials (logo, colors, fonts) or is this from scratch?
- Who will be the main point of contact on your side?

Once I have these, I'll send you a formal proposal within 48 hours.

[Your name]

---

## 2. Project proposal

---
Subject: Proposal — [Project Name] / [Your Name]

Dear [Name],

Following our discussion, please find my proposal for [project description]:

**Scope:** [3–5 bullet points of what's included]

**Timeline:** [Start date] to [End date], [number] weeks

**Investment:** ₦[amount]

**Payment terms:** 50% upfront, 50% on delivery (transfer to [account details] or via invoice)

**What I need from you:** [list of assets/information needed to start]

This proposal is valid for 14 days. To proceed, please reply confirming your acceptance and I'll send the invoice for the initial payment.

[Your name]

---

## 3. Project kickoff

---
Subject: [Project Name] — We're starting

Dear [Name],

I've received the initial payment — thank you. We're officially underway.

Here's what happens next:
1. I'll send you the first draft/milestone by [date]
2. You'll have [number] days to review and provide feedback
3. We'll have [number] revision rounds included in the scope

If anything changes on your end that might affect the timeline, please let me know as early as possible.

Looking forward to building this with you.

[Your name]

---

## 4. Milestone delivery

---
Subject: [Project Name] — Milestone [#] ready for review

Dear [Name],

[Milestone name] is ready for your review. Here's what I've completed:

[List of deliverables / link to the work]

To view: [access link / password if applicable]

What I need from you:
- Your feedback by [date] so we stay on schedule
- Approval to proceed to the next phase

If you have questions before then, I'm available [your availability].

[Your name]

---

## 5. Revision request response

---
Subject: Re: Revisions for [Project Name]

Dear [Name],

Thank you for the feedback. I'll incorporate the following changes:

[List what you're changing — be specific]

The revised version will be ready by [date].

One note: [If any requests are out of scope, flag them here: "Item 3 (adding the new section) is outside our original scope — I can add it for an additional ₦X,000 or defer it to a phase 2 if you'd prefer to keep the budget as planned."]

[Your name]

---

## 6. First payment reminder (before due date)

---
Subject: Payment reminder — Invoice #[number]

Dear [Name],

This is a friendly reminder that Invoice #[number] for ₦[amount] is due on [date].

Payment details: [Account number / payment link]

If you have any questions about the invoice or if something has changed, please let me know.

[Your name]

---

## 7. Late payment follow-up (7+ days overdue)

---
Subject: Overdue invoice — [Your name] / [Project name]

Dear [Name],

Invoice #[number] for ₦[amount] was due on [original due date] and is now [X] days overdue.

I wanted to check that everything is okay on your end and that the invoice was received. If there's a problem with the payment or you need to discuss the timing, please let me know directly — I'd rather work something out than have this linger.

If I don't hear from you by [specific date], I'll need to pause work on [any ongoing work] until the account is settled.

[Your name]

---

## 8. Project completion

---
Subject: [Project Name] — Final delivery

Dear [Name],

[Project name] is complete. Here's what you're receiving:

[Final deliverables list / links / access]

To close out:
- The final invoice (#[number], ₦[amount]) is attached
- If you used any accounts/services set up during the project, I've documented the credentials in [location]
- [Any handover instructions]

It's been a pleasure working on this. If you need anything in the future, I'm here.

[Your name]

---

## 9. Referral request

---
Subject: A quick favour

Dear [Name],

I'm so glad [project name] went well — your [specific thing they said / result] meant a lot to hear.

I work almost entirely through referrals, and if you know anyone facing a similar [website/design/development] challenge, I'd be grateful for an introduction.

I'll take good care of anyone you send my way.

[Your name]

---

## 10. Price increase notice

---
Subject: Update to my rates from [date]

Dear [Name],

I wanted to give you advance notice that I'll be adjusting my rates from [date]:

[Current rate] → [New rate]

This reflects [brief explanation: time in the market / cost of tools / demand].

Any projects booked and invoiced before [date] will be at current rates. I'm very much looking forward to continuing to work together.

[Your name]
