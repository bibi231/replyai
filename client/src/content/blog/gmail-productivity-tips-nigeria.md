Gmail is the dominant business email client in Nigeria. Most people use it at 20% of its capability. Here are the features that actually move the needle.

## 1. Schedule Send

Send emails at the optimal time without being at your desk. Right-click the Send button → "Schedule send." Useful for:
- Sending at 8am when you're writing at midnight
- Delivering important emails at the start of the recipient's working day
- Managing time zones if you work with international clients

## 2. Snooze emails

An email you can't deal with right now doesn't need to clutter your inbox. Click the clock icon → snooze until a specific time. It disappears from your inbox and reappears when you need it.

This is one of the highest-value inbox management features most people ignore.

## 3. Undo Send

Gmail gives you up to 30 seconds to cancel a sent email. Go to Settings → "Undo Send" → set to 30 seconds. This has saved many careers.

## 4. Templates (canned responses)

Enable "Templates" in Settings → Advanced. Create templates for:
- Your standard project proposal
- Your payment follow-up
- Your meeting request
- Your onboarding email

These are better than typing from scratch every time, but ReplyAI is better still — because templates don't adapt to context.

## 5. Multiple inboxes

Settings → Inbox → Multiple Inboxes. You can create separate panes for:
- Starred (high priority)
- Snoozed
- Specific labels (clients, internal, follow-up)

This prevents important emails from getting buried.

## 6. Keyboard shortcuts

Enable in Settings. The most useful:

| Shortcut | Action |
|---|---|
| `c` | Compose new email |
| `r` | Reply |
| `e` | Archive |
| `#` | Delete |
| `/` | Search |
| `g + i` | Go to Inbox |
| `g + s` | Go to Starred |
| `.` | Open more options |

Learning 5 shortcuts saves 30+ minutes per week for heavy email users.

## 7. Search operators

Gmail search is extremely powerful. Useful operators:

- `from:name@domain.com` — emails from specific person
- `to:me is:unread` — unread emails sent to you directly
- `has:attachment larger:5M` — large attachments (find and delete to free storage)
- `before:2025/01/01 is:unread` — old unread emails (bulk clean)
- `in:inbox label:important` — important emails in inbox

## 8. Confidential mode

For sensitive information (financial details, contracts, personal data), use Confidential Mode. The recipient can't forward, copy, print, or download the email. You can also set an expiry date.

## 9. Gmail offline

Install Gmail Offline Chrome extension. Read, reply, and compose emails without internet. Changes sync when you reconnect. Essential for Nigerian professionals dealing with unstable connections.

## 10. Priority Inbox

Settings → Inbox → Priority Inbox. Gmail learns which senders and subjects matter to you and surfaces them above everything else. Combine with keyboard shortcut `g + i` to jump straight to your most important emails.
